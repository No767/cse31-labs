1. Optimization and redundancy. If we added those, then we would need to make the CPU itself more complex to support them. Besides, we can inverse the original instructions to make it work:

    A < B is just slt A, B followed by bne (branch if true).

    A > B is just A < B with the registers swapped: slt B, A followed by bne.

    A <= B is the exact opposite of A > B. So, you just do slt B, A and use beq to branch if it's false.
